INSTRUCTIONS FOR PLAYING:

游놓Your task is to fly from Cuba to Norway to retrieve an ancient ingredient and then transport the ingredient back to Cuba.

游놓Each round, you choose the next airport you want to fly to from three automatically selected options.

游놓The selectable airports appear as dots on the map, and by pressing the "fly" button, you can fly to your desired destination.

游놓The prices of plane tickets vary depending on the destination.

游놓The game has a limited amount of currency and distance to aliens.

游놓If you run out of currency or distance, you lose the game.

游놓You can earn more currency by completing optional tasks at the airports. Tasks open by pressing the task button. A wrong answer costs you one distance step to the aliens.

游놓If you manage to return to Cuba with the ingredient, you have won the game.
